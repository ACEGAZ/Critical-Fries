There were once 3 adventurers who each had tremendous power! There was Robert, a wizard who's skill rivaled even the mighty karsus. Reece who traveld the realm in search of enlightenment to increase his martial prowess. And finally Gary, who crafted incredible weapons to overpower his foes.

They had all been on many quests together and overcome insurmountable odds, but as they stood atop their towers they each had a revelation. The power they had gained could be used for more then slaying their enemies. They looked around their homeland at the starving citizens and knew what they needed to do.

And thus, Critical Fries was born!
